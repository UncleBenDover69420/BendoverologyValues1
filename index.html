What are the values?

There are four independent aspects - Main Branch, Devotion to Ben Dover, Sexuality & Identity, and Societal Role - and each has two opposing values assigned to them. They are:

TRADITIONALISM

Traditional Bendoverologism is the first branch of Bendoverology, which used to be the dominant one. Traditionalists strictly follow the rules of the Rapeble.

MAIN BRANCH

REFORMISM

Reformist Bendoverologism is the newer branch of Bendoverology, which spread to become the more popular one. Reformists still believe in Ben Dover, but don't always abide by the Rapeble.

RELIGIOUSITY

Some people are more religious than others, making them more devoted to Ben Dover.

LIBERTY

Some people believe that personal liberty is more important than Ben Dover's words.
